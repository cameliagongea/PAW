About I Was There

This is a web application, which helps people to find a restaurant based on price, restaurant type and dish, serving and best food.

The application's users can use it to find a restaurant, may require adding a new restaurant in a certain category and send reviews.

The restaurant owners will use the web portal to send real information about their restaurant, for example a description of the restaurant, contact information and their menu.
